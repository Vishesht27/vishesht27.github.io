Shifting to Mumbai from Delhi

Date: January 15, 2024 | Reading Time: 10 min | Author: Vishesh Tripathi

It's been 14 days since I moved from Delhi to Mumbai.


Pros


  • Clean Air: A refreshing change compared to Delhi, specially if you compare AQI ðŸ¥²
  • Stable Weather: The climate is constant and predictable.
  • Tourist Spots: Plenty of nearby places to visit, like Lonavala.
  • Coastal Views: Being near the seashore offers some amazing views.
  • Great Food: Both Delhi and Mumbai are known for their delicious cuisines.

Cons


  • Humidity: The weather can feel uncomfortably humid at times.
  • Metro Connectivity: Compared to Delhi, Mumbai's metro system is less extensive, so traffic can be a challenge.

Overall Experience


Both cities are Tier-1, so you don't face major issues like a lack of services or facilities.


Unfortunately, I haven't had much opportunity to explore Mumbai due to my working hours (1 PM to 1 AM). Sundays are usually reserved for rest.


Still, I managed to visit Amar Juice Centre, famous for its Pav Bhaji. I also made a quick trip to Lonavala (my third visit overall) to meet a friend, traveling to and from in a single day.
